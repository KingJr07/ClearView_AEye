Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can the AI model help with early detection of eye diseases?

    Our AI ClearView model analyzes retinal images and patient data to detect eye conditions with a level of accuracy. It assists ophthalmologists in providing early diagnoses and personalized treatment plans.

  • Can the AI model help with early detection of eye diseases?

    Yes, the AI model excels at early detection, helping identify issues like myopia and diabetic retinopathy before symptoms manifest, which is crucial for timely treatment.

  • What if I need to locate an ophthalmologist near me?

    You can easily find ophthalmologists in your area through our website. Tap on more details of the ophthalmologist and get the location feature to discover eye care specialists conveniently close to you.

  • How can I communicate with an ophthalmologist before my appointment?

    Our web app offers a chat page where you can engage in conversations with ophthalmologists. It allows you to ask questions, discuss concerns, and gain insights before your appointment.

  • Can I schedule appointments through the website?

    Absolutely, you can schedule appointments with ophthalmologists directly through our platform. Just select your preferred specialist and book an appointment that suits your schedule.
